Kinematic Equations

There are 4 equations used to solve projectiles. These equationsuse the same 5 variables:

  • v- final velocity
  • a- accelration due to gravity
  • vo=- initial velocity
  • Δx- x/y displacement

    • It is important to note that when you are solving an equation you need to dolve for x and y sepratley. That means in most cases it is necessary to solve for the same variable twice.
